About Melt Me

Candles made with intention, care, and love.

Our Story

Melt Me was created from a genuine love for candles and the quiet moments they create. What began as a personal passion became a way to slow down, be present, and bring warmth into everyday spaces.

Each candle is thoughtfully made with intention—from the careful selection of scents to the final pour. Lighting a candle is a small ritual, a pause in the day, a moment of calm.

Every Melt Me candle is crafted with care, simplicity, and heart—meant to be felt as much as it is seen.
